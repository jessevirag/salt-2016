About Us

Salt is a branding studio based in Ottawa, Canada’s capital. We create identities; brands that are clear, authentic, simple, and connect with your audience.

Services

  • Art Direction
  • Logo Design & Branding
  • Web Design
  • Advertising
  • Illustration
  • Motion Design & Animation
  • Packaging
  • UI/UX
  • Photography
  • Signage
  • Book Design
  • Newsletters

We work with organizations of many sizes and industries, who are trying to refocus their voice, or find it. We treat every job individually: from quoting, to hand-picking a team according to what’s needed. We work with clients who value creative and practical ideas, and appreciate the direct collaboration. We join forces with them, forming tight relationships that help us find a shared vision.

To do this, we will ask you the hard questions. We’ll challenge you and push you further, to make sure you truly understand what your brand is and where it needs to go, distill your ideas and hone your message. Our process questions, researches, experiments and explores. We like to get our hands dirty, learn new things, and stay nimble.

Does it sound like we'd make a good match? Let's meet. The first coffee is always on us.
